Explore Amazonian Regions in Detail

  • Amazon River

    • Highlighted Attractions: Explore the expansive river and its tributaries, witness stunning sunsets over the water, and discover remote indigenous communities along its banks.
    • Popular Activities: River cruises, fishing excursions, wildlife watching (including pink river dolphins), and cultural visits to riverside communities.

  • Manu National Park, Peru

    • Highlighted Attractions: Encounter iconic species like giant river otters, jaguars, and colorful macaws. Explore the Manu Cultural Zone to learn about the traditions of indigenous communities.
    • Popular Activities: Guided hikes through diverse ecosystems, wildlife photography, birdwatching tours, and cultural exchanges with local communities.

  • Yasuni National Park

    • Highlighted Attractions: Explore the lush rainforest canopy on elevated walkways, spot unique wildlife like howler monkeys and toucans, and visit the Yasuni Biosphere Reserve.
    • Popular Activities: Canoe excursions along blackwater rivers, night safaris to spot nocturnal creatures, cultural visits to indigenous communities, and birdwatching expeditions.
