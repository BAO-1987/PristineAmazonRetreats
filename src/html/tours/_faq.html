FAQs: Your Antarctic Adventure Questions Answered

  • How do I book a tour with Pristine Amazon Retreats?

    Booking a tour with us is easy! Simply visit our website, choose your preferred tour package, select your dates, and follow the prompts to complete your booking securely online.

  • What types of accommodations are provided during the tours?

    We offer a range of accommodations, from comfortable lodges nestled in the rainforest to cozy river cruises aboard our boats. Each option ensures a relaxing stay amidst the beauty of the Amazon.

  • What should I pack for an Amazon tour?

    Essential items include lightweight and breathable clothing, sturdy footwear for jungle treks, insect repellent, sunscreen, and a reusable water bottle. Check our detailed packing list for more guidance.

  • Are the tours suitable for families with children?

    Yes, we offer family-friendly tours tailored to accommodate various age groups. Our guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, with activities suitable for children and adults alike.

  • What safety measures are in place during the tours?

    Your safety is our priority. We adhere to strict safety protocols, employ experienced guides, and provide necessary equipment for all activities. Emergency plans are in place to ensure a secure and worry-free experience.

  • Can dietary restrictions be accommodated during the tours?

    Absolutely! Please inform us of any dietary requirements or allergies when booking your tour. Our team will make every effort to accommodate your needs and ensure you enjoy delicious meals throughout your journey.
