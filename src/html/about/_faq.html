faq

  • What safety measures are in place for Antarctic expeditions?

    Safety is our top priority. Our expeditions are led by experienced guides who are trained in first aid and emergency protocols. We conduct thorough safety briefings before each excursion, carry comprehensive medical kits, and have emergency evacuation plans in place. Additionally, our vessels and equipment are meticulously maintained to ensure the highest safety standards.

  • How do I book a tour and what are the payment policies?

    Booking a tour with us is straightforward. You can reserve your spot through our website or by contacting our customer service team. We require a deposit at the time of booking, with the balance due 90 days before departure. We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and bank transfers. Detailed terms and conditions, including our cancellation policy, are provided during the booking process.

  • What should I pack for an Antarctic expedition?

    Packing for Antarctica requires careful preparation. We provide a comprehensive packing list tailored to your specific tour. Essential items include thermal clothing, waterproof outer layers, sturdy boots, and sun protection. We also recommend bringing high-quality camera equipment to capture the stunning landscapes and wildlife. Our team is available to answer any specific questions you might have about gear and clothing.
