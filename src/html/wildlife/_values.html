Plant Life and Ecosystems

  • Tropical Rainforest Ecology

    Learn about the layers of the rainforest: canopy, understory, and forest floor, each supporting distinct ecosystems and biodiversity. Understand the interdependence of plant and animal species within these layers, which contributes to the rainforest's resilience and diversity.

  • Featured Plant Species

    Discover key plant species such as giant kapok trees, orchids, bromeliads, and medicinal plants. Explore their ecological roles, from providing habitat and food sources to their medicinal and cultural uses by indigenous communities.

  • Unique Ecosystems

    Highlight unique Amazonian ecosystems like flooded forests (várzea and igapó), savannas (campinas), and riverine habitats. Understand the adaptations of plants to seasonal flooding, nutrient-rich soils, and diverse microclimates within these specialized environments.
